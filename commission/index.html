Custom allows you to choose the individual features, like Subtitles or Color Correction, while bundle includes all 4 for $10 off.

DURATION

The duration of your main source footage, or the total duration of clips. If you have separate audio/video recorded, enter the video duration. If it's only audio being sent, enter the duration of the audio.

SUBTITLES

You will be sent subtitle files, which can be uploaded with the video to YouTube or embedded in the video itself.

COLOR CORRECTION

Color correction for footage; Better looking footage tuned to make colors pop.

BGM/SFX

Additional sound effects and music will be added, does not necessarily have to include both.

SHORT
