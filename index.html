WHO WE ARE

Nebula Studios is a multimedia content creation studio dedicated to making just plain cool stuff.
We're mostly comprised of neurodivergent queer nerds who just wanna create
We're the creators of projects including The Meatloaf Theorists, the VIOLET Assistant, the Nebula Heads Up Display System, and plenty of unpublished releases in the works ;)

THE MEATLOAF THEORISTS

The Meatloaf Theorists is a satire channel based on the channels created by Theorist Media/Matthew Patrick and owned by LunarX. The Meatloaf Theory project was intended to create a well edited parody of the Theory channels, copying the editing and production style as closely as possible on a 0 budget.
