THE CRUCIFIER

RAILGUN MK I PROTOTYPE

Status: Completed

Date: 10/9/24

The Crucifier is a sci-fi railgun design that fires 5 inch nails at a target using electromagnets. This was 3D modelled mostly from scratch. Exceptions include the laser attachment, NVIDIA Jetson, foregrips and relays.


Software used: Blender, Adobe After Effects & Illustrator, Handbrake
